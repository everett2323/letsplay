Cross Road Unblocked 76

Crossy Road Unblocked 76 is an unblocked version of Crossy Road in which players can play online anywhere. The player's task is to help a chick cross the road and move forward. To do that, you need to avoid dangerous obstacles: vehicles, rivers, trains...

How to play Crossy Road Unblocked 76

Overcome dangers

The chick may encounter various dangers in the game, including vehicles, rivers, and eagles. For vehicles on the highway and swift rivers, players need to time their movements properly and move properly to protect the chick. For the eagle, you need to move continuously so that he does not catch the chicks.

Collect gold coins

To unlock other hilarious characters and advance in the game, remember to collect gold coins as you move. However, many times, gold coins can be in dangerous positions; at that time, prioritize the safety of the chick.

Try different characters

You can try changing characters to feel the newness and excitement of the game. Each character will bring its characteristics, helping you have different experiences and discover many new approaches to overcome challenges. Changing characters also increases diversity and does not make the game boring.

Crossy Road Unblocked 76 is a wonderful game with beautiful graphics and humorous sounds. Get ready to enter this challenging but also extremely lovely world of the Crossy Road series of games, one of which is Crossy Road Castle.
